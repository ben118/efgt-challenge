How it Works

First, think of your number one temptation zone. Is it your co-worker’s desk, your drive home when you pass a fast food restaurant, or the corner coffee shop with your favorite latte? Whatever it is, identify it, and come up with your strategy for avoiding it, so junky food doesn’t trigger you during this most vulnerable time.
