Overview

Welcome to the Eat Fat, Get Thin 21­ Day Plan. We are so excited that you have taken this step towards better health! This plan is all about busting our fear of fat while following a plan that brings you greater mental clarity, weight loss, balanced energy, deeper sleep and overall better health. Before we start day one, there are a couple of steps that we recommend you take so that you are prepared to start your program.

Step 1: Establish Your Baseline: Take the Quizzes and Intake Survey

Before you embark on the Eat Fat, Get Thin journey, it’s important to establish where you are starting from and prepare yourself for the 21­day trek by taking the FLC and the Diabesity / Carbohydrate Intolerance Quizzes. You will also retake these quizzes at the end of the program. We guarantee you will be amazed at the changes in just three short weeks.

We also would really appreciate if you could take our short intake survey. The information you provide will help us continue to improve the program and make sure we continue to help you achieve your goals. Also, make sure to check with your doctor before you do this program.

Step 2: Kitchen Makeover

During this stage of the plan, take some time to detox your kitchen of the items in the list below. Don’t just tuck these away – toss them in the garbage! If you have a moment of panic, rest assured: we’re going to quickly and easily replace them with delicious, healthy alternatives that will leave you more than satisfied.

  • Anything that is not real food and processed foods.
  • All foods that contains sugar
  • Any foods with artificial sweeteners, preservatives, additives and coloring or dyes
  • Any food or drink that contains sugar or any kind
  • Anything that contains hydrogenated oils and refined vegetable oils

The following items also have to go, but if you feel uncomfortable throwing them away, just put them out of sight during the 21­day plan, as long as you feel you can safely avoid them.

  • All products containing gluten
  • All grains
  • All dairy products
  • All beans
  • Any food or drink that contains sugar or any kind
  • Anything that contains hydrogenated oils and refined vegetable oils

We guarantee that if you make time to detox your kitchen before you start the plan, you will feel much more prepared to start and will feel ready for success beginning on day one.

Checklist

  • PGX supplement with large glass of water 15 minutes before breakfast
  • Detox shake
