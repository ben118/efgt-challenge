Overview

Now that we have figured out how to nourish our bodies and have started the detox process, we want to focus on energizing​ the body! I recommend that everyone continue walking at least 30 minutes a day. Exercise is not a strategy for weight loss alone, but it has a thousand other benefits, such as:

  • Helping with insulin sensitivity
  • Reducing stress
  • Boosting metabolism
  • Improving brain health
  • Reducing the risk for chronic disease
  • Enhancing detoxification
  • Slowing the aging process
  • Improving sexual function
  • Regulating your mood

Dr. Hyman has said many times in the past that you can’t exercise your way out of a bad diet.But exercise combined with a high­-quality diet will help build muscle and speed up your metabolism.

Here are 3 strategies to working exercise into your daily routine:

  1. Make it a priority and think ahead. ​Human nature dictates that we are more likely to exercise if we’ve made it easy for ourselves. Get your sneakers out of the closet, choose whatever clothing you’ll be most comfortable in for walking, and make sure it’s clean and accessible. Let’s get the obstacle of preparation out of the way so you’re ready to lace up and go.
  2. Do it first thing in the morning. ​We all know how easy it is to push exercise to the back burner and subsequently find reasons to not do it. Do your daily exercise first thing in the morning to jumpstart your metabolism and set your day on the right course.
  3. Make it fun. ​Getting into great shape doesn’t mean hours at the gym, plodding along on elliptical machines or suffering through classes lead by punishing drill­-sergeant­-like instructors (unless you like that kind of thing!). Try to explore all the fun things you may enjoy that involve movement and choose those. Save exercise time for when you just can’t find a way to play on your own or with someone else. Vary up your routine and above all, make it fun.

Another focus for Week Two is reducing your stress levels and finding better ways to cope with stress. When you are stressed it triggers your body to gain weight ­ especially belly fat. Not only that but stress ramps up your cravings and disturbs your sleep patterns.

So this week, let’s focus on getting in daily movement and don't forget to make it fun!

Daily Checklist

Morning

  • Walk or do 30 minutes of exercise
  • Take PGX just before breakfast with large glass of water
  • Take your supplements with breakfast
  • Take your MCT oil
  • Make your shake or breakfast
  • Drink water (at least 4 glasses throughout the morning)
  • Add at least 1 capful of E­-Lyte to 8 ounces of water

Afternoon

  • Take PGX just before lunch with large glass of water
  • Eat lunch
  • Drink water (at least 4 glasses throughout the afternoon)

Evening

  • Take PGX just before dinner with large glass of water
  • Eat dinner
  • Take your second dose of supplements with supper
  • Add second capful of E­-Lyte to 8 ounces of water
  • Do one or more of your relaxation practices (can be done any time of day)
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon potato starch in water before bed
  • Relax: Take time to unplug
  • Get 7 to 8 hours of sleep
